Paper Submission

Submissions must follow the guidelines provided on the official SAC conference website. The review process is double blind, and all manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format.

  • Maximum length: 8 pages (up to 2 additional pages allowed with extra fee, for a total of 10)
  • Manuscripts must be anonymized for double-blind review
  • Original, unpublished work only; no double submissions
  • Accepted papers must be presented at the conference (in-person or proxy)
  • Papers not presented will be excluded from the ACM Digital Library
